Chicken Parm

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Basil
  • (Optionally, buy premade pomodoro or marinara sauce)
  • Vegetable/canola oil
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Chicken breast
  • Parmesean cheese
  • Panko breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Cook onions and garlic down until soft, translucent, and sweet
  2. Add crushed tomatoes + basil to mixture, and cook until sauce is nice and thick
  3. Bash chicken breasts until they are 1/4 inch in thickness. Then coat fully with flour, then egg, and finally, the breadcrumbs (with garlic powder + parmesean)
  4. Fry the chicken cutlets until golden brown and perfectly crisp
  5. Place some sauce then mozzarella cheese on top of each cutlet, and broil for a few minutes. Enjoy
